===============================
No Payment Info in Website Shop
===============================

Update the checkout process templates to better suit the situation
where no payments are made immediately during the checkout process.


Payment page in checkout:
-------------------------
* Hide payment method selection below product listing

Confirmation page in checkout:
------------------------------
* Hide payment information below product listing
* Change the term `Pay Now` to `Confirm`
